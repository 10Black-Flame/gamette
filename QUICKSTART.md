# ⚔️ Chronicles of Aetheria - Quick Start Guide

Welcome, brave adventurer! This guide will help you get started with your epic fantasy journey.

## 🚀 Getting Started (30 seconds)

### Step 1: Open the Game
- **Option A (Easy)**: Double-click `story.html` 
- **Option B (With Server)**: Run `node server.js` then visit `http://localhost:3000/story.html`

### Step 2: Create Your Character
1. Enter your hero's name
2. Choose your class:
   - 🛡️ **Warrior** - Strong and tanky (if unsure, pick this!)
   - 🔥 **Mage** - Powerful spells
   - 💨 **Rogue** - Fast and sneaky
   - ✨ **Cleric** - Healer
3. Click "Start Adventure"

## 🎮 Basic Controls

### Exploration
- Click on **NPCs** to talk or shop
- Click on **Enemies** (red text) to fight
- Click **Exits** to move to new areas
- Use **Map** button to see all locations

### Combat
| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| ⚔️ Attack | Normal damage (costs nothing) |
| 🔥 Cast Spell | High damage (costs MP) |
| 🛡️ Defend | Reduce incoming damage |
| 🏃 Flee | Try to escape (40% success) |

### Inventory
- **Item** - Use to heal or restore MP
- **Equipment** - Equip weapons/armor for bonuses
- Hover over items to see their effect

## 📍 Where to Go

### For Beginners
1. Start in **Aldermere Village**
2. Talk to NPCs to learn about quests
3. Visit the **Blacksmith** (Thorin Ironforge) to buy equipment
4. Visit the **Alchemist** (Elara Moonwhisper) to buy potions
5. Head to **Dark Forest** to fight goblins

### Recommended Path
```
Village → Forest (fight goblins) → Cave (harder goblins)
→ Mountains (trolls) → Sky Tower (golems)
→ Castle (undead) → Dungeon (final boss: Shadow Lord)
```

## 💡 Tips for Success

### Combat Tips
- ✅ Use Defend against strong enemies to reduce damage
- ✅ Buy healing potions before tough fights
- ✅ Cast spells when you have extra MP
- ✅ Flee if you're losing (low HP)
- ❌ Don't waste all your gold on items

### Character Building
- **Warrior**: Focus on fighting tough enemies directly
- **Mage**: Use spells for heavy damage, keep mana potions ready
- **Rogue**: Fast attacks, good for hit-and-run tactics
- **Cleric**: Can heal yourself, good for survival

### Gold Management
- 💰 Collect items from defeated enemies and sell them
- 💰 Use gold to buy better equipment
- 💰 Don't hoard - spend strategically
- 💰 Harder enemies drop more valuable items

## 📊 Key Stats Explained

| Stat | What it Does |
|------|-------------|
| **HP** | Health - when it reaches 0, you lose |
| **MP** | Mana - used for spells |
| **Attack** | Damage dealt to enemies |
| **Defense** | Damage reduction from enemy attacks |
| **Speed** | How fast you act in combat |
| **Gold** | Currency to buy items |
| **Experience** | Earn to level up and become stronger |
| **Level** | Shows your progression (max 99) |

## 🎯 Main Quest: Defeat the Shadow Lord

The ultimate goal is to defeat the **Shadow Lord** in the **Castle Dungeon**. To prepare:
1. ⬆️ Level up to at least level 10
2. 💰 Save at least 500 gold
3. 🛠️ Equip best gear from the blacksmith
4. 🧪 Stock up on healing potions
5. ⚔️ Practice combat in easier areas first
6. 📜 Get the main quest from Brother Isaiah in the temple

## ⚡ Quick Start Strategies

- Focus on exploring all locations

- Complete side quests for gold

### Combat Master Route
- Fight every enemy you encounter

### Treasure Hunter Route
- Collect all available items

## 🎓 Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Engaging too many enemies at once  
❌ Using all your potions early

❌ Not saving regularly  
❌ Ignoring NPCs and their dialogue

## 🆘 Stuck? Here's Help

### Low on Health
- Visit the **Innkeeper** (Marta) to rest
- Use **Heal spell** if you're a Cleric
- Use **Healing potions** from inventory

### Too Many Enemies
- **Flee** from battle!
- Come back when stronger
- Try a different area

### Need Gold?
- Collect and sell items
- Fight easier enemies for quick rewards
- Check locations for free items

### Can't Find Something?
- Click the **Map** button
- Check all NPCs in locations
- Read quest descriptions
